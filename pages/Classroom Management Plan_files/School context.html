Demographics and Teaching Mode

Demographics

Student Profile

Adult learners from different cultural backgrounds, ages 18-65.

All students have similar English levels (A2-B1)

They come from various countries and bring rich life experiences to the classroom.

Many work full-time or part-time jobs and attend classes in the evening or weekends.

Classes happen through video conferencing. Students join from their homes, which means they have different setups and comfort levels with technology.

The online format requires clear communication and simple procedures that work well on screen.

Classes are conducted via Zoom or similar platforms, utilizing:

  • Breakout rooms
  • Interactive whiteboards
  • Chat functions
  • Collaborative digital tools (Google Workspace, Microsoft Teams)

Backup Plan

To ensure accessibility for all students:

  • Low-bandwidth tasks
  • Phone-friendly slides
  • Captions on videos
  • Alternative communication channels
